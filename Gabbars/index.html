Our Experts


Chef Vikentiy Ushakov

Food is the epicenter for Vikentiy’s life – what inspires him every day. It’s the way he makes a living, the way he relaxes, the way he express himself, and how he keeps himself healthy. He communicates with the world, and experiences the world, through food.

Chef Luis Sanchez

Our Executive Chef Luis Sanchez is the culinary heart and soul of Abuelo’s. Born and raised in Mexico, it was there that Chef Luis developed his passion for cooking, spending countless hours in the kitchen with his grandmother and learning the techniques and recipes that he still uses today


Chef Alexander Roberts

Alex was born in Buffalo, New York, but his formative years were spent here in Minneapolis. He started working in local kitchens in high school and through college. In the early 1990’s, Alex moved to New York City, where he attended the French Culinary Institute. After graduation, Alex gained experience in several renowned New York restaurants, including Gramercy Tavern, Bouley, and the Union Square Cafe.
